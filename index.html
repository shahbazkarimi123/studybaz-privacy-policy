Study Baz ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Study Baz mobile application.

The app itself does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The camera is used only for scanning documents to create PDF files. All scanned content is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

The app requires storage/media access to save generated PDF files and downloaded images to your device so you can view or share them later. Files remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

No personal information is stored or processed by the app itself. We take reasonable measures to protect user data handled by third-party services.
